## Product Principles

1. Tianma Work OS is not a chatbot.
2. Human owns strategy; AI handles execution support.
3. Multi-model value comes from role collaboration.
4. Complex decisions activate AI Board Mode.
5. Final product hides context compression and indexing from users.
6. Domain-specific cockpits are required.
7. Professional service-first design is the goal.
8. Important problems follow a first-principles loop.

## First-Principles Loop

```text
What is the essence of the problem?
Why are we doing this?
Can we solve it?
Yes.
How do we solve it?
Is there a better way?
```

There is no best, only better.
