# AI Team Roles v0.1

## Purpose

This document defines initial AI team roles for Tianma Work OS.

The innovation is not model aggregation. The innovation is role-based AI collaboration.

## Core Roles

- **Commander** — clarifies the goal and produces the final recommendation.
- **Strategist** — evaluates direction, trade-offs, timing, priorities, and long-term implications.
- **Research Analyst** — gathers and interprets evidence.
- **Data Verifier** — checks source reliability, data freshness, contradictions, and source-of-truth hierarchy.
- **Risk Officer** — identifies downside, failure modes, invalidation conditions, exposure limits, and approval needs.
- **Product / Solution Designer** — turns problems into product modules, workflows, cockpits, or system designs.
- **Execution Secretary** — converts decisions into tasks, checklists, documents, file structures, or execution steps.
- **Review Officer** — reviews prior decisions, outcomes, scores, and lessons learned.
- **Meta-Strategist** — improves the decision framework and collaboration process itself.

## AI Board Mode

Activate AI Board Mode when the decision is high-risk, data is uncertain, sources disagree, roles may disagree, product direction is affected, or the decision affects money, legal risk, safety, reputation, or public release.

## AI Board Output

1. Decision question
2. Context summary
3. Known facts
4. Unknowns and assumptions
5. Role opinions
6. Consensus
7. Disagreement
8. Risk review
9. Source verification status
10. Final recommendation
11. User approval requirement
12. Review trigger
