AI Decision Team

Roles are derived from the 2026WC workflow.

Each role shows the input it needs, output it produces, and review signal used to improve the next run.

赛果复盘员

Responsibility: compare actual results with prior predictions.

Input: actual score, prediction record, settled result.

Output: hit/miss review and correction notes.

Review signal: direction, score, totals, and upset accuracy.

赔率盘口分析师

Responsibility: interpret user-supplied market notes.

Input: 1X2, handicap, totals, score odds, consensus notes.

Output: market layer and suspicious pricing flags.

Review signal: pricing read vs match outcome.

战术风格分析师

Responsibility: evaluate style, formation, pressing, transition, and set pieces.

Input: team tactics and match-up notes.

Output: tactical path and match state assumptions.

Review signal: whether the expected match pattern appeared.

球员状态分析师

Responsibility: assess form, injuries, rotation, load, and key-player condition.

Input: user-supplied roster and availability context.

Output: player-condition adjustment.

Review signal: whether player condition changed match quality.

历史与大赛心理分析师

Responsibility: weigh history, pressure, and tournament psychology.

Input: history, knockout pressure, rivalry, and big-match context.

Output: pressure-risk adjustment.

Review signal: overreaction or underreaction to big-match effects.

爆冷审计员

Responsibility: challenge main picks and flag upset candidates.

Input: underdog path, odds tension, tactical mismatch.

Output: upset watch and confidence warning.

Review signal: upset hit/miss and missed warning signs.

总指挥

Responsibility: synthesize roles into final daily recommendations.

Input: all role outputs and user feedback.

Output: final picks, priority, and compact sync.

Review signal: final decision quality and rule drift.

Prediction Layer
