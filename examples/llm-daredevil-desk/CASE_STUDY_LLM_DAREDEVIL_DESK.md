# Case Study: LLM Daredevil Desk v0.1

## Purpose

LLM Daredevil Desk is the first seed battlefield for Tianma Work OS.

It is a real high-frequency trading-analysis project used to stress-test Tianma Work OS under high-risk, data-intensive, time-sensitive conditions.

## What LDD Tests

- Source-of-truth hierarchy
- Risk Officer role
- Market Analyst role
- Data Verifier role
- Strategy vs historical position separation
- Robot/grid-bot position tracking
- Forecast review and scoring
- Decision logs
- Multi-account de-duplication
- Net exposure mapping
- Opposite exposure detection
- Position intent tagging
- Cross-project DUXD feedback

## Public Safety Note

This public case study must not include private screenshots, private account identifiers, raw trading logs, private financial account details, or unsanitized execution records.

## Product Conclusion

LDD proves that Tianma Work OS needs domain-specific cockpits, decision protocols, memory systems, and review loops. Generic chat is not enough for professional work.
