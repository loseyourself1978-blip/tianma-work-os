# Product Vision v0.1

## Core Thesis

AI must move from answering questions to executing goals.

Tianma Work OS is a multi-model AI team command system that turns goals, problems, documents, decisions, and projects into structured execution.

## Product Category

Tianma Work OS is not a chatbot, a generic agent builder, a normal workflow automation tool, or a developer-only project manager.

It is a **multi-model AI team command system**.

## Why It Exists

Serious work requires memory, decisions, assets, requirements, reviews, risk control, domain-specific state, cross-project handoff, and human approval.

## Product Principles

### Human Strategy, AI Execution

The user owns strategy, direction, judgment, approval, and accountability.

AI handles research, structuring, drafting, verification, execution support, memory retrieval, review, and continuous improvement.

### Multi-Model Role Collaboration

The innovation is assigning different AI roles and models to the work they are best suited for.

### AI Board for Complex Decisions

High-risk, strategic, or conflicted decisions should use AI Board Mode.

### User-Invisible Context Management

The final product should automatically compress, index, archive, retrieve, and restore context in the background.

### Domain-Specific Cockpits

A trading project, product project, legal project, and healthcare project should not share the same generic template.

### Professional Service-First Design

The product should deliver extreme professionalism, service, execution, and intelligence.
